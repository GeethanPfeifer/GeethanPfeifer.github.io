Magritte

How to play

Magritte is a game about trying to maximize profit by buying a company's future (uncertain) profits for a fixed value.

Each round, you will see a graph which is a PDF of a company's profit, in units of 1, which is conveniently also the value of that company. (Each round is a different company; a company's value does not carry over.)

You will bid 'X' for this profit, and a company will accept any offer such that the logarithm of the company's value after taking this offer is greater than the expected value of the logarithm of the company's value assuming they don't accept this offer.

It is assumed we have an infinite bankroll, so our goal is simply to maximize the EV of our profits (difference between our bid and the EV of the compny if they accept, 0 otherwise), by bidding as close as we can to a company's threshold without going lower than it.

"Error" is our "missed" profits (EV).
